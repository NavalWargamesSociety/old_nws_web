BATTLEFLEET

 

 

 

 

 

 

Battlefleet is the NWS' journal, which is almost entirely constructed from members' contributions. Any aspect of naval wargaming / naval history (with a view to wargaming) is likely content, however there are no quotas etc. of period etc. for each issue. In addition it also contains model news, and reviews of new releases, with a section for members to advertise/ post queries for free.

 

Battlefleet is available both in hard copy and as a password protected PDF electronic copy
